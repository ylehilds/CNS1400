         CNS 1400: Lab Six

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Objectives:
The objective of this lab is to help you understand how to write and use your own functions. 

After completing this lab, you should be able to come up with solutions to problems that use programmer written functions. You should be able to write a function and invoke a function. You should understand the principle of stepwise refinement to help you solve computational problems.
