Objectives:
The objective of this lab is to build your understanding of pointers, and to give you practice using pointers in a C++ program. 

After completing this lab, you should be able to 

Explain what a pointer is. 
Discuss some of the major issues involved with the use of pointers. 
Write a program that uses pointers and pointer arithmetic. 
Explain the special relationship between pointers and arrays.
